
## Cylindrical

**cylindrical to cartesian**

$$
\begin{align}
x &= \rho \cos(\theta) \\
y &= \rho \sin(\theta) \\
z &= z
\end{align}
$$

**cartesian to cylindrical**

$$
\begin{align}
\rho &= \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \\
\theta &= \tan^{-1}(y/x) \\
z &= z
\end{align}
$$
**volume element**
$$
dV = r\ dz\ dr\ d\theta
$$

## Spherical

**Spherical to cartesian**
$$
\begin{align}
x &= \rho \sin (\phi) \cos (\theta) \\
y &= \rho \sin (\phi) \sin (\theta) \\
z &= \rho \cos (\phi)
\end{align}
$$

**cartesian to spherical**
$$
\begin{align}
\rho^2 &= x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \\
\theta &= \tan ^{-1} (y/x) \\
\phi &= \cos ^{-1} (\frac{z}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}})
\end{align}
$$
**volume element**
$$
dV =  \rho^2 \ \sin (\phi) \ d\rho \ d\phi \ d\theta
$$
