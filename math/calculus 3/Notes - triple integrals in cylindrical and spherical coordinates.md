
## Cylindrical

**cylindrical to cartesian**

$$
\begin{gather}
x = \rho \cos(\theta) \\
y = \rho \sin(\theta) \\
z = z
\end{gather}
$$

**cartesian to cylindrical**

$$
\begin{gather}
\rho = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \\
\theta = \tan(x/y) \\
z = z
\end{gather}
$$
**volume element**
$$
dV = r\ dz\ dr\ d\theta
$$
